Overview

The AnimeScheduler is an application that will aid you in organizing all of the anime you have watched on a simple application so you don't forget that different shows you watch on a seasonal basis.


Features

  • Ability to see all the shows you are watching or have watched
  • Ability to see the anime shows you have saved for later in the backlog
  • Ability to see shows you watch on a daily basis
  • Ability to add a show to the list of all shows you watch
  • You can add, delete or update a show you are watching/about to watch

Where to start?

Check out the navbar on the top to go through the different menu options.
